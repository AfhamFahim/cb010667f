About

Wilpattu National Park is the largest national park in Sri Lanka, spanning over 1,317 square kilometers. It is renowned for its natural beauty, diverse wildlife, and ancient ruins.

Wildlife at Wilpattu

Embark on a journey through the lush landscapes of Wilpattu and witness the majestic elephants, elusive leopards, colorful birds, and a myriad of other fascinating creatures in their natural habitat.

Plan Your Visit

Immerse yourself in the wonders of Wilpattu National Park. Plan your visit to experience guided safaris, camping under the starlit sky, and the thrill of encountering wildlife up close. Be prepared for an adventure of a lifetime!
